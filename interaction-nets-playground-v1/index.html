Quick Guide

Edit Mode:

  • Add Cell: Click a symbol in the inspector and double-click the canvas.
  • Add Wire: Drag from one port to another.
  • Add Loop: Shift-click a port.
  • Drag: Drag a cell or free port.
  • Rotate: Scroll while hovering over a cell, or drag its rotation handle.
  • Delete: Select cell/wire/free-port and press Delete or Backspace.

Simulate Mode:

  • Active wires (linking two principal ports) glow in purple.
  • Click Step or Play to reduce the net.
  • Watch the physics layout automatically clean up the wires.
